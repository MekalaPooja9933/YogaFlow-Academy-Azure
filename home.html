Types of Yoga

Acro Yoga

It stretches and relax your body, improves stamina and memory

Vinyasa Yoga

It serves as a training to increase strength and help to build lean muscle mass throughought the body.

Hatha Yoga

It introduce beginners to the main relaxation techniques and Asans

Kundalini Yoga

It helps relax and restores the mind and the body, improving strength, flexibility and endurance

Mantra Yoga

your mind releases the positive energy that decreases the negative thoughts or stress. Chanting mantras is an ancient practice that calms your mind and soul.

Asthanga Yoga

It can improve flexibility, balance, and strength, and can also detoxify the body. Jivamukti yoga can also help you learn about scripture and techniques that can lead to a relaxed mind and soul.
